KMReader - Native Komga Reader for iOS & macOS

KMReader is a full-featured Komga client with dedicated comic and EPUB readers. Browse, manage, and enjoy your comics, manga, BDs, magazines, and novels across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

KEY FEATURES

• Unified Library Management: Browse Series, Books, Collections, and Read Lists with advanced search, filtering, and sorting that remember your layout preferences.
• Personalized Dashboard & History: Keep Reading, On Deck, Recently Added, Recently Updated, and infinite-scroll reading history make it easy to jump back into any book.
• Reader Modes for Comics & Webtoons: LTR, RTL, vertical, and adjustable-width webtoon reading, dual-page spreads with optional cover skipping, pinch-to-zoom, customizable tap zones, auto-hide controls, page jumps, and page exports (JPEG/PNG/HEIF/WebP).
• EPUB Reader Powered by Readium: Full-book EPUB downloads cached for offline or incognito reading, choose any installed font or the publisher default, adjust font size, toggle paged vs. continuous scroll, pick auto/single/dual-column layouts, and switch between system, light, sepia, or dark themes with a live chapter navigator.
• Complete Progress Tracking: Automatic sync across devices, resume from the last page, in-progress indicators, and incognito mode when you want to read without leaving a trace.
• Collections & Read Lists: Build curated collections, add/remove entries, and manage your backlog from anywhere.
• Server Controls & Smart Caching: Admin tools for server info, metrics, scans, metadata refresh, and trash management alongside a configurable 512MB–8GB cache for instant image and EPUB loading—even offline.
• Native macOS Experience: Dedicated reader window, keyboard shortcuts, and full feature parity with iOS.
